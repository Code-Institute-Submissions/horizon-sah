About Us

Horizon is a service that focuses on creating bespoke landscape art and photography works from the Scottish Highlands. We produce our own work and offer an art commission service (see the Custom Artwork link above) for those looking for something personal.

Established in 2020, and based near Inverness, the Highland landscape from its rugged moorlands and moody skies to stunning beaches and glorious sunsets, has been our single biggest influence to showcase our work through a range of different media.

Please feel free to contact us with any queries, feedback or just general comments.
